## Discovery Questions

*Answer these questions before implementation starts*

### 1. Problem Definition
- What is the core problem we're solving?
- Who is the end user or stakeholder?
- What are the success criteria?

### 2. Scope & Constraints
- What's included in this scope?
- What's explicitly excluded?
- Are there time or resource constraints?
- Any technical dependencies or limitations?

### 3. Architecture & Design
- What's the high-level architecture?
- What design patterns will we use?
- Are there existing patterns we should follow?
- How will this integrate with current systems?

### 4. Implementation Strategy
- What's the step-by-step implementation plan?
- What are the major milestones?
- What are potential risks and mitigation strategies?
- How will we measure progress?

### 5. Testing & Validation
- What testing strategy will we use?
- What are the acceptance criteria?
- How will we validate success?

### 6. Knowledge & Learnings
- What new learnings might we acquire?
- How should we document this for future reference?
- What should go into CLAUDE.md?
